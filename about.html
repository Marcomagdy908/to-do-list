About This Site

Welcome to the To-Do-List app!

This site helps you organize your daily tasks in a simple and interactive way. You can create groups for your tasks (like Today, Tomorrow, or any custom group), add tasks to each group, and check them off as you complete them. You can also edit or delete tasks and groups, and see your progress with a fun percentage indicator and confetti when you finish everything!

The app saves your tasks in your browser, so you won't lose them when you refresh or close the page. You can also switch between light and dark mode for a comfortable experience.

Get started by adding your own tasks and enjoy staying organized!
